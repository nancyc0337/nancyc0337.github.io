Movie Webpage Project

Context: Wednesday, 11/8/2023

During this Movie Webpage Project, we apply our knowledge about HTML(HyperText Markup Language) and CSS(Cascading Style Sheets) to make a website about a fictional movie.

Content: My groupmates (Kyle Lin, Zoe Chi) and I made a movie project. The title of the movie webpage is Computer Virus. We chose horror to make our movie. It's about a group of teenagers exploring and came across an abandonded school. They found a computer, which has a virus, and they got sucked in. In order to escape, they have to play the game of Detective.

Reflection:

Challenges: The challenging part of this project was the story. The story took time to write out. It taught me about Time Management(prioritizing and scheduling tasks) & Growth Mindset(patience, perseverance).

Takeaways:

* communicating: during this project, my groupmates and I communicate to ask for help and to ask each other about how's each other's doing.

* collaboration: during this project, my groupmates and I collaborate togther to get a good grade and to finish this project together.

* debugging: while communicating, my groupmates and I learn how to debug/fix our errors in our code.

Next steps: If we had more time, we would make the style of the webpage a bit more horrific. If we had more time, we would also added a sequel.
