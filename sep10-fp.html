SEP 10 Freedom Project

Context: Monday, 6/3/2024

The "Freedom Project" for SEP10 is a year-long project all about making a website that informs the viewer of the current and future innovations in the topic of my choosing.

Content: For my project, I chose the topic of Music. I used HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Github. I also chose Aframe to independently study Music in order to help me make by website.
