CYOA (Choose Your Own Adventure) Project

Context: Thursday, 2/8/2024

During this Choose Your Own Adventure Project, we apply our knowledge about command lines, git commands and merging(Automatic Merging & Merge Conflict) to make an adventure project.

Content: My groupmate (Kyle Lin) and I made a adventure project. This adventure we made is about you are stuck in a school. In this adventure, you have to find a way to escape this school by choosing different paths that comes with different outcomes.

Reflection:

Context: My teacher assign this project because this project help us practice using command lines and git commands in our IDE. We also get to practice merging(Automatic Merging & Merge Conflict) and communication.

Challenges: The challenging part of this project was communication. During semester 1, I was in period 6 with my partner Kyle Lin. But during semester 2, I got moved into period 5 while my partner is in SEP in period 6. So I had to find a way to communicate with him to get the work done. I use platforms like Gmail to contact, leave him a message of what I did and ask him any questions he has.

Takeaways:

* communicating: during this project, I have to practice communicating with my partner especially my partner and I aren't in the same class together. Communicating helps us to know what we need to complete and what we worked on.

* collaboration: during this project, my groupmates and I collaborate togther to get a good grade and to finish this project together.

Next steps: If we had more time, we would maybe add more text, images and files to our existing adventure story.
